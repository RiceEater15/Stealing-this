eSign and Scarlet are both apps that let you install other apps (IPAs) on your iPhone without needing the App Store. Here's how they work: when you have an IPA file, both eSign and Scarlet can "sign" it with a certificate. This certificate acts like a digital ID card, telling your iPhone that the app is safe to install and use. When you use eSign or Scarlet to sign an IPA, they create a special code called a signature, which ensures that the app hasn't been altered. After it's signed, you can install the IPA on your iPhone, and the signature convinces your device that the app is safe to run.
